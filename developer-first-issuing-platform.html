The default banking-as-a-service platform will be developer-first [kunle.app]

The developer-first default

  • The default addresses most developer use cases from the very beginning
  • For the use cases that the default doesn't solve, either the developers won't need them at their current state, or those are something that they can build for themselves

Examples of developer-first default businesses

  • Starting an online store → Shopify
  • Collecting payments → Stripe
  • Sending SMS → Twilio

The value of becoming a developer-first default in fintech

  • Fintech is becoming embedded within software products
    • Shopify's merchant payments (using Stripe)
    • The Uber Card
    • Lyft's Direct Debit Card
  • High retention, engagement, and switching costs
    • Example: When a checking account is issued to an engaged customer:
      • Salary is deposited to the account
      • Bills are linked to and being paid through it
      • The card is being used for purchases
